# kid-03 — Story dice

An app that picks 3 random words from a list to start a story. The simplest of the kid trio — pure DOM, a button, three text slots, and `Math.random()`. Designed to baseline FAST-engine time-to-MVP: if even this trivial dream takes longer than ~5 minutes in FAST, the bench surfaces a regression in the engine itself rather than in dream complexity.
